P R O F E S S I O N A L H I S T O R Y

Crown & Champa Resorts February 2001 –Present

ASSISTANT FOOD & BEVERAGE MANAGER, Kuredu Island Resort FROM JULY 2017 - Present

  • Overall in charge of operations in all Food Service Outlets: Ala Carte, Room Service, Special Events, Buffet Restaurants, Staff Restaurants, Staff and Banqueting Other Events
  • Dealing with the suppliers on daily basis and building up a good connection with them.
  • Implementing new designs for the outlets
  • Implementing new SOPs
  • Implementing new system to keep the track on guest’s satisfaction.
  • Interviewing to hire new staffs
  • Keeping the records of all the staffs
  • Evaluating all the supervisors and staffs
  • Preparing allocation, items, decoration and promotion for Christmas and New Year
  • Checking daily, monthly and annual revenue and implementing new ideas to increase the revenue of food and beverage.
  • Checking the cost of items specifically every single item and finding better product with cheaper price.
  • Implementing new ideas to change the ambience of all the outlets.
  • Training staffs and Supervisor to male them confident and reliable.
  • Approving fusion & Paytex
  • Approving staffs weekly plan
  • Approving material control (MC)
  • Maintaining monthly cost
  • Finding solution to sell nonmoving products. Creating new cocktail.
  • Taking care of all the fixed assets
  • Promoting special events
  • Implementing new special dinners
  • Making a track on slow moving beverages and nonmoving beverages. And replacing those with alternate product.
  • Maintaining all the inventories
  • Taking full responsible of whole department absence of F&B Manager
  • Taking F&B Manager responsibilities in his absence.
  • Taking full responsibilities of Restaurant Manager
  • Taking full responsibility of F&B Operations Manager.
